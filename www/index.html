The Media Lab


Web solutions, mobile apps, software and more... all hand made in our cosy Worcester based lab.

What we do

Designing and developing great ideas

Worcester Media Lab is a student led design and development lab working in conjunction with the University of Worcester.

Bursting with personality and technical expertise our team posses a wide range of skills, and we love nothing more than flexing our creative muscles!

How we do it

Sustainable practices and solutions

At the Media Lab we believe that the web should be a beautiful, functional and fun place. We design elegant solutions to client based problems, and try and do our bit towards creating a better web experience for visitors - and your business with sustainable practices and solutions.

We love a challenge - and have an incredibly broad range of expertise and skills to form a solution that is right for you. We use modern and cutting edge development practices with a splash of the traditional old pen and paper too!
