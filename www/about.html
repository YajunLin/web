About Us


Our cosy lab shrouded in a world of history

The Worcester Media Lab

The Worcester Media Lab is a University of Worcester initiative for students of the school to gather, discuss and work together to create awesome innovations in the world of web for our community and businesses.

The Media Lab is supported by University staff, however the business is led and run by students of the University. Students can join the Media Lab and access a world of creativity, learning and experience working with businesses locally and nationally.

We work with businesses, charities and other organisations - locally and nationally, small and large - to develop creative and sustainable solutions, covering all aspects of the web including mobile technologies.

We're located within the beautiful University of Worcester City Campus, a historic Georgian Infirmary that has been one of Worcester’s most important buildings for over 200 years.

Our Core Values

We are Creative

The Media Lab is formed of current students at University of Worcester, with a broad range of talent and skills that mixed together make the perfect recipe for a masterpiece. We strive to achieve the very best and our diversity encourages creativity and learning within our team, as well as our clients.

We are Passionate

Our skills might be broad but we always come together in our passion for the web, our projects and clients. We take deep pride in our work, and we're always keeping up to date with the latest developments in the web, as well as employing some traditional best practises.

We are Enthusiastic

It’s rarely quiet in our cosy lab - the sound of keystrokes, discussions about newest design trends or even the latest amazing web technology. We are complete workaholics and perfectionists, so we won't stop until you're happy.

We are Sustainable

We know how important sustainability is to business, and we utilise sustainable practises wherever possible in our work and coding. We also offer sustainable solutions when it comes to hosting and technology platforms too.
